Why Register as a Donor?

This site is a community-run donor directory. We collect volunteer donor information so people in need can quickly find nearby donors and connect with them directly.

When someone submits a request for blood, registered donors in the specified area receive an email notification so they can respond if they're available. This helps families and hospitals locate donors faster.

Registering is quick and keeps you part of a local network of lifesavers. Please remember this platform is community-run and not a substitute for professional medical services.

LifeBlood is not partnered with hospitals or medical providers — always verify medical guidance with a qualified professional.

How the Directory Works

Register

Create a donor profile with your contact details, blood type, and city so people nearby can find you.

Set Availability

Mark where you're available to donate. Donate blood in that area in the time of need.

Receive Notifications

When a request is submitted for your area and blood type, you'll receive an email with the request details so you can decide if you can help.

Connect & Coordinate

If you can help, contact the requester directly to arrange the donation details and timing.
